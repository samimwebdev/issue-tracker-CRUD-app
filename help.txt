Frontend
    - what we see during browsing a website
    -Tech (HTML, CSS, JAVASCRIPT)
    -user interaction (dropdown on hover, animation on scroll)


Backend- 
    - what's happened beyond the view
    - registering user 
    - Login user 
    - giving access based on special access 
    - saving into database, securing business logic
    - programming language (JAVA, c#, JAVASCRIPT, python)



CMS- content management system
    - providing content (As raw data or visually polished template)
    - Regular website, laravel, django, express etc 

Raw data - Headless CMS
